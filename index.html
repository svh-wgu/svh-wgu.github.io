Discover Taniti

Tucked away in the Pacific, Taniti is a small yet stunning island brimming with natural beauty and cultural charm. Spanning less than 500 square miles, the island boasts diverse landscapes, from white sandy beaches and rocky shores to lush rainforests and a breathtaking mountainous interior crowned by an active volcano
Most visitors find themselves drawn to Taniti City, the island’s vibrant hub, where native architecture meets the tranquil waters of Yellow Leaf Bay. Beyond the city, adventure awaits—whether it’s a guided boat or bus tour around the island, an immersive hike through the rainforest, or an awe-inspiring visit to the active volcano.
Once a quiet island sustained by fishing and agriculture, Taniti is now an emerging travel destination that perfectly balances tradition, relaxation, and adventure. Whether you're here to explore or unwind, Taniti offers an unforgettable island experience.

See, Do, Explore.

  • Things to Do

  • Swim & Sunbathe

    Enjoy the ultimate beach day with refreshing swims in crystal-clear waters and plenty of time to sunbathe on golden sands. Whether you’re looking to unwind or soak up the sun, the island’s serene beaches offer the perfect escape for relaxation and rejuvenation.

  • Surf

    Catch the perfect wave with a thrilling surfing session on the island’s stunning beaches. Ride the crystal-clear waters, feel the rush of the surf, and enjoy the island’s laid-back beach atmosphere. With breathtaking views and ideal surf conditions, this adventure offers the ultimate combination of excitement and relaxation for your vacation!

  • Ua Ngahere Rainforest

    Venture into the heart of Ua Ngahere Rainforest and immerse yourself in its lush greenery and diverse wildlife. Wander through winding trails, discover hidden waterfalls, and experience the tranquil beauty of one of the island’s most captivating natural wonders. Perfect for nature enthusiasts, this adventure offers unforgettable sights and sounds of the rainforest.

  • Puia Volcano

    Discover the power and beauty of Puia Volcano, where you can explore volcanic craters, witness steaming vents, and enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape. Whether you’re hiking to the summit or simply taking in the dramatic scenery, this natural wonder offers an unforgettable adventure for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike.

  • Snorkeling & Diving

    Dive into crystal-clear waters and discover vibrant coral reefs, exotic marine life, and stunning underwater landscapes. Whether you're snorkeling along the shore or diving deeper into the ocean, the island offers unforgettable experiences for both beginners and experienced enthusiasts. Explore the hidden beauty beneath the surface and make memories that will last a lifetime.

  • Eat

    Indulge in the island’s vibrant food scene with fresh, locally-sourced dishes at charming restaurants. From seafood delights to traditional island fare, each meal offers a taste of authentic island culture. Enjoy a cozy atmosphere, friendly service, and unforgettable flavors that will make your vacation even more memorable.

  • Golfing

    Get ready for an exciting golfing experience at our upcoming world-class golf course, opening next year. Set against breathtaking landscapes, this course will offer challenging fairways and stunning views for golf enthusiasts of all levels. Stay tuned for the grand opening and be among the first to tee off at this incredible new destination!

  • Lunar New Year

     29 January

    Join in the vibrant celebrations of Lunar New Year, where traditional customs come to life with colorful parades, delicious feasts, and joyful festivities. Experience the excitement of dragon dances, red lanterns, and family gatherings, marking the start of a prosperous new year. It’s a time for reflection, renewal, and celebrating togetherness with friends and loved ones.

  • Matari’i i Raro

     20 May

    Celebrate Matari’i i Raro, a unique festival that honors the island's cultural heritage and natural abundance. This vibrant event features traditional dances, music, and rituals that connect the community with the land and sea. Enjoy local delicacies, witness captivating performances, and experience the deep-rooted customs that bring the islanders together in joy and unity.

  • The Heiva i Taniti

     4-20 July

    Experience the vibrant Heiva i Taniti, a lively festival celebrating the island’s rich traditions and cultural heritage. Enjoy captivating performances of traditional music, dance, and storytelling, along with colorful parades and local crafts. This annual event brings the community together to honor the island’s history and celebrate its vibrant culture in a joyful and unforgettable way.

  • Matari’i i Ni’a

     20 November

    Immerse yourself in the vibrant festivities of Matari’i i Ni’a, a celebration of abundance and prosperity. This cultural event honors the harvest season with traditional music, dancing, and feasts showcasing the island’s rich heritage. Experience the joy of community gatherings, enjoy delicious local dishes, and take part in rituals that celebrate nature's bountiful gifts. It’s a time for renewal, gratitude, and celebrating the island’s vibrant culture.
